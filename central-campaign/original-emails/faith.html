Loma Linda University students find faith within our school.

We believe in health for the whole person: mind, body and soul — that’s why an essential piece of the learning experience at Loma Linda University is nurturing your own spiritual health. Doing so allows room for growth and the ability to enhance the care you provide to your patients.

You’ll learn to develop your spirituality and use it in the care you provide.

PRAY

Learn how and when to pray with patients to bring strength and comfort.

CONNECT

Use a powerful whole-person care model to connect provider, patient and God.

UNIFY

Attend chapel weekly to recenter your faith and connect with the campus family.

As one of a select few faith-based academic medical complexes in the nation, our goal is to fully integrate faith into our education. Students from all eight of our schools come together to attend classes on important aspects of religion in healthcare, building bridges between different disciplines and learning to harness the power of spiritual care.
