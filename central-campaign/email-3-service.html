When you’re a student here, service opportunities matter.

At Loma Linda University, we offer lots of options for students interested in mission and outreach. If you’re passionate about serving others with hands-on learning, we can help you do that.

Whether it’s a short trip or a long stay, you can grow from the experience of serving abroad in your field. If you would like to stay closer to home, our CAPS community partnership program offers great opportunities for serving locally.
