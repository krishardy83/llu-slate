LIVING LONGER

Since 1958, the Adventist Health Studies have helped to uncover why Loma Linda is one of several Blue Zones™ worldwide where people live longer, healthier lives.

INFANT HEART TRANSPLANTS

Leonard L. Bailey, MD performed the first infant heart transplant here in 1984, leading to hundreds of successful transplants at our children’s hospital and around the world.

FIGHTING CANCER

In 1990, James M. Slater, MD opened the first hospital-based proton therapy program, allowing for critical research into cancer radiation therapy and computerized procedures.
